Optimization Over Lunch

Conversations that improve models — and minds.

This course offers a rare and exclusive learning experience: lunch with our guest speakers at the Stanford Faculty Club, where ideas unfold in small circles rather than lecture halls.

Each lunch is:

  • Free
  • Limited to five students
  • Designed for curiosity and dialogue

These lunches create space for thinking aloud, asking questions you can’t ask elsewhere, and exploring how ideas evolve. Seats are few by design — to keep the conversation meaningful.

How It Works

  • Choose one or more lunches you’d like to attend.
  • Seats are filled first-come, first-served.
  • Preference may rotate so all interested students have a chance.
  • Bring questions, perspectives, and a willingness to think together.
