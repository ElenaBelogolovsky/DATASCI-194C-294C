Talk Title

Benchmarking AI and the role of technical standards in growing AI markets

Abstract

This lecture describes the importance of benchmarks and other technical standards for growing AI markets, with a focus on benchmarks and standards developed by the non-profit MLCommons Association. It covers the MLPerf hardware benchmarks, AILuminate reliability benchmarks, MedPerf federated evaluation framework, and the Croissant data standard.

For each benchmark or technical standard, the lecture provides the industry context behind its creation, a technical overview, forward development challenges, and its utility across academic research, large-scale industry deployments, and startups.
