2. Assignments (5 total) — 45%

Each assignment contributes 9% to the final grade.

The capstone synthesizes conceptual, technical, and applied elements from the course.

4. Final Presentation — 15%

Students will give a short presentation during Week 10 "Leaderboard Day."
