Grading

This course offers two grading tracks. Students must select one track early in the quarter.

Track I: Capstone Track

(For students who plan to complete a capstone project)

1. Attendance & Participation — 15%

A. Attendance Evaluation

Attendance is based on the number of class sessions attended (out of 20 total classes) and is calculated according to the following scale:

Classes Attended Evaluation Percentage Earned
18–20Excellent15%
16–17Very Good14%
14–15Good13%
12–13Satisfactory12%
10–11Low Pass11%
8–9Poor10%
6–7Very Poor9%
0–5No Credit0%

B. Class Participation

A portion of this grade is based on active engagement in classroom discussions.

C. Guest Speaker Lunch Participation (Required)

  • Each student must participate in at least four (4) lunches with guest speakers during the quarter.
  • Students must arrive prepared with thoughtful questions and actively engage in discussion.

2. Assignments (5 total) — 45%

  • Five assignments total
  • Each assignment contributes 9% to the final grade

3. Capstone Project — 25%

The capstone project synthesizes conceptual, technical, and applied elements of the course. Detailed guidelines and expectations will be provided separately.

4. Final Presentation — 15%

Students will give a short presentation during the final class session (“Leaderboard Day”), presenting their capstone work.
